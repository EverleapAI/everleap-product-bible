# Product Flow

## Purpose

Everleap is an evidence-driven developmental platform.

The product is designed around a continuous cycle of collecting evidence, generating understanding, encouraging action, and learning from new experiences.

Unlike a traditional assessment, Everleap never reaches a final result.

The system continuously updates its understanding as new evidence becomes available.

---

# The Product Loop

The entire product can be described as one continuous loop.

```
Evidence
    ↓
Scientific Interpretation
    ↓
Cross-Science Understanding
    ↓
Guidance
    ↓
Action
    ↓
New Evidence
```

Every feature in Everleap contributes to one or more parts of this loop.

---

# Step 1 — Evidence Collection

Everything begins with evidence.

Evidence comes from multiple sources.

Current evidence sources include:

- Onboarding conversations
- Story responses
- Micro tasks
- Daily reflections
- User actions
- Future product interactions

The purpose of evidence collection is not to score users.

It is to understand them.

Every new piece of evidence should increase understanding rather than increase certainty.

---

# Step 2 — Scientific Interpretation

Raw evidence has little value until it is interpreted.

Each scientific framework independently analyzes the evidence assigned to it.

Current sciences include:

- Ikigai
- Enneagram
- What Color Is Your Parachute

Future sciences may include:

- Self Determination Theory
- Big Five
- Narrative Identity
- Possible Selves

Each science answers one unique question.

Each produces a structured science memo.

No science produces user-facing content directly.

---

# Step 3 — Cross-Science Synthesis

Science memos become the foundation for higher-level understanding.

Rather than asking one science to explain the whole person, Everleap compares observations across multiple sciences.

Agreement increases confidence.

Disagreement creates useful questions.

This synthesis produces cross-science hypotheses rather than conclusions.

These hypotheses power:

- Insights Summary
- Today
- Recommendations
- Future guidance

---

# Step 4 — Guidance Generation

Once sufficient understanding exists, Everleap generates guidance.

Guidance should always be:

- contextual
- evidence-based
- timely
- actionable
- humble

Examples include:

- Today's guidance
- Reflection prompts
- Tiny Tasks
- Insight summaries

Guidance should encourage thinking.

It should never replace thinking.

---

# Step 5 — User Action

Understanding only becomes valuable when users do something with it.

Everleap encourages users to:

- notice
- reflect
- experiment
- practice
- revisit
- observe

Every action becomes another opportunity to learn.

Small actions repeated over time produce meaningful growth.

---

# Step 6 — New Evidence

Every interaction generates additional evidence.

Evidence updates science memos.

Updated science memos improve synthesis.

Improved synthesis creates better guidance.

The cycle repeats continuously.

The product becomes increasingly useful because it learns alongside the user.

---

# Background Generation

Everleap intentionally separates user interaction from AI generation.

Pages do not call language models directly.

Instead:

1. User creates evidence.
2. Generation requests are queued.
3. Background workers process requests.
4. Science memos are updated.
5. Product pages read generated content from the database.

This architecture provides:

- consistency
- explainability
- reliability
- scalability

The user experiences fast page loads while background intelligence continues improving.

---

# Product Layers

Everleap operates across four conceptual layers.

## Layer 1 — Evidence

Collect observations.

No interpretation.

---

## Layer 2 — Science

Interpret evidence through individual scientific lenses.

No user-facing guidance.

---

## Layer 3 — Synthesis

Combine scientific observations into broader developmental hypotheses.

No career predictions.

No labels.

---

## Layer 4 — Experience

Deliver guidance through:

- Today
- Story
- Insights
- Explore
- Recommendations

The experience layer should always feel conversational and supportive rather than analytical.

---

# Success

The product flow succeeds when:

Evidence becomes understanding.

Understanding becomes reflection.

Reflection becomes action.

Action becomes new evidence.

The system improves with every cycle without ever pretending to reach a final answer.

Everleap is designed to become wiser over time—not because the AI becomes smarter, but because the relationship becomes richer.
